Exhibitions

Solo Art Exhibition: "Visions in Color"

Date: January 15, 2025

Location: Kalakriti Art Gallery, Hyderabad

A solo exhibition featuring Ravi Chitrakar's exploration of vivid abstract landscapes. Each artwork reflects the intersection of emotion and nature. Visitors will also enjoy a guided tour by the artist on the opening night.

Tickets:Available online or at the gallery

Group Exhibition: "Art Beyond Borders"

Date: March 12, 2025

Location: National Gallery of Modern Art (NGMA), Bengaluru

This international group exhibition brings together artists from around the world to celebrate cultural diversity through contemporary art.

Entry:Free for students and museum members; ₹20 for general admission

Upcoming Events

Live Painting Session: "Art in Action"

Date: February 23, 2025

Location: Jehangir Art Gallery, Mumbai

A live painting event where Ravi Chitrakar creates a masterpiece in real time while sharing insights into their creative process. Attendees can interact with the artist, ask questions, and enjoy an inspiring day of art.

Workshop: "Exploring Abstract Techniques"

Date: May 19, 2025

Location: India Habitat Centre, New Delhi

A one-day interactive workshop hosted by Ravi Chitrakar, focusing on abstract painting methods, including brush techniques, color blending, and creating texture. Ideal for beginners and professionals alike, the workshop includes all necessary materials and a certificate of participation.

Registration: ₹50 per person. Pre-booking is required.
